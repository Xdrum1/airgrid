NEW FEATURE

Causal Intelligence Layer

Every score is now traceable to a source event. Three new components on every city page:

Factor-Level Citations
Each scoring factor shows the specific event or filing behind it — linked to source, with verification date.
Score Timeline
Every score change annotated with date, delta, which factors moved, and the triggering event. See exactly why a score changed.
"What If" Scenario Modeling
Toggle factors on and off to see projected score and tier changes in real time. What happens to Phoenix if SB1826 passes?
METHODOLOGY

Weather Infrastructure — USDOT Rationale Added

The Weather Infrastructure factor now cites the USDOT AAM National Strategy as its basis — weather is one of four federally recognized infrastructure pillars alongside physical, energy, and spectrum. This rationale is now in both the methodology page and the March report.
