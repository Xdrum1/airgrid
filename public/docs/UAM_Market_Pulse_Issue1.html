LEAD STORY

Miami, FL Hits 100 — A Perfect UAM Readiness Score

Miami, FL became one of the U.S. cities to achieve a perfect score on the AirIndex UAM Market Readiness Index this week, jumping from 80 to 100. Miami, FL now holds all seven readiness factors: an active pilot program, an approved vertiport, active operator presence, vertiport zoning, a favorable regulatory posture, state legislation, and full LAANC airspace coverage.

Why it matters: Markets achieving top-tier readiness scores carry a credibility advantage in operator route planning and infrastructure investment decisions.
